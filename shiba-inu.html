Shiba inu

ORIGIN
Japan
GROUP
Non-Sporting
SIZE
Medium

The Shiba Inu is a small to medium-sized dog breed from Japan, originally bred for hunting in mountainous terrain. Known for its fox-like appearance, it has a curly tail, upright ears, and a thick double coat that comes in colors like red, sesame, black and tan, or cream. Shibas are alert, independent, and spirited, often displaying a bold and confident personality. Despite their stubbornness, they are clean, intelligent, and loyal to their owners, making them a popular companion dog worldwide.
